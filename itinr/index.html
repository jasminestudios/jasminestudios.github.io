Itinr

Your whole trip in one place.

Itinerary, calendar, documents, expenses and to-dos — together. Drop in your booking PDFs and photos and let AI build the plan for you.


✈️ One tidy itinerary

Flights, hotels, transport and activities laid out by day — with a calendar view and a map of where you're going.

🤖 AI does the typing

Add a booking PDF or a screenshot and Itinr extracts the events automatically. It can even suggest things to pack and do.

📄 Documents & tickets

Keep passports, reservations and tickets attached to your trip, ready offline when you need them.

💰 Expenses

Scan receipts, group by day or category, and convert currencies so you always know what you've spent.

✅ To-dos & reminders

Track packing lists and bookings with local reminders — no account, no server required.

🔒 Private by design

Your data stays on your device and in your own iCloud. Prefer fully on-device AI? That option sends nothing out.

Share & export

Share a read-only copy of a trip with travel companions via iCloud, or export the whole itinerary — attachments included — as a self-contained HTML file. Available in 11 languages.
