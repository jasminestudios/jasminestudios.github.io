Questions, answered.

Do I need to create an account?

No. There's no sign-up and no login. Your trips are stored on your device and in your own iCloud — Itinr's servers never store your trip data.

Where is my travel data stored?

On your device and in your private iCloud database, synced across your devices. AI requests are passed through for processing only and never retained.

What can I do for free?

Everything — planning, recording, and the recap — plus an AI taste every month. With an Apple Intelligence iPhone or your own API key, AI stays free too.

Is Trip Pass a subscription?

No — it's a one-time purchase for 30 days (your first pass lasts 60). No auto-renewal. When the trip's over, it's simply over.

Can I share a trip with family or friends?

Yes — share via iCloud for viewing or editing together, or send a live web link anyone can open. Pro includes Family Sharing.

Does it work offline?

Viewing your itinerary and recording work offline. The offline trip pack (Trip Pass/Pro) keeps every photo and document on your device in one tap — and on-device AI even works in airplane mode.
